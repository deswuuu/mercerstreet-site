What we offer

Full-range care,
every stage of life.

Book a Consultation
General Dentistry
Routine check-ups, customized cleaning schedules, fillings, and gum disease treatment — tailored to your oral health goals and designed to keep problems from developing in the first place.
→
Cosmetic Dentistry
Professional whitening and custom porcelain veneers to create a brighter, more uniform smile. We evaluate your individual anatomy to find the option that delivers lasting, natural-looking results.
→
Dental Implants
A long-term solution for missing teeth. We assess candidacy at your visit and discuss all restoration options — implants, crowns, and more — so you can make the right choice for your situation.
→
Invisalign
Clear, removable aligners custom-made using 3D imaging. Treats crowding, gaps, overbites, and more — virtually invisible, fully removable, and designed to fit seamlessly into daily life.
→
Emergency Care
Broken tooth, lost filling, severe toothache? We keep urgent slots in our schedule and will do our best to see you the same day. Call us as soon as possible — we'll take it from there.
→
TMJ & Sleep Appliances
Custom oral appliances for TMJ pain and snoring or mild sleep apnea. We identify the underlying cause and, where needed, coordinate with your sleep specialist to find a lasting solution.

The team

Meet your
dental partners.

Dr. Sara Hansen
Dentist & Principal
A graduate of Northwestern University Dental School (1998) following studies at McGill, Dr. Hansen has practised in downtown Toronto for over 25 years. Known for her compassion, clear communication, and partnership approach to care. Outside the office she enjoys singing, ceramics, and gardening.
Dr. Rachel Park
Dentist
DDS from the University of Toronto, Bachelor of Health Sciences (Honours) from McMaster. Dr. Park takes time to build genuine connections with every patient, combining a gentle touch with thorough, personalized care. Off duty she plays tennis and explores new Toronto restaurants.
